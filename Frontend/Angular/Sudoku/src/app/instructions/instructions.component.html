How to Play Sudoku

Welcome to the AKM Sudoku game! Sudoku is a logic-based puzzle where the goal is to fill in the grid with numbers 1 through 9.

Game Rules

  • The game board is a 9x9 grid, divided into 3x3 subgrids.
  • Each row, column, and subgrid must contain all numbers from 1 to 9 without repeating.
  • Some numbers are pre-filled in the grid; your task is to fill in the rest.

How to Play

  1. Click on an empty cell and enter a number from 1 to 9.
  2. Use logical deduction to determine which numbers belong in each cell.
  3. Avoid guessing; each puzzle has a unique solution with no guessing required.

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with rows, columns, or subgrids that have the most numbers filled in.
  • Use the process of elimination to narrow down the possibilities for each cell.
  • Take your time, and don't rush! Accuracy is more important than speed.
